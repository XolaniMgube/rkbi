About Us

Rapula Kgomotso Business Enterprises (RKBI) is a registered petroleum wholesaler, importer, and exporter operating since 2011, focused on delivering reliable energy solutions.

We supply high-quality petroleum products to aviation, commercial, and industrial clients, while also providing professional construction services that support infrastructure development across the region.
